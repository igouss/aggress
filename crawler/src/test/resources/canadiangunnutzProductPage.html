    Lee Enfield EAL.3 digit serial. Mint

    For Sale: Excellent condition Lee Enfield EAL rifle, commercial variant with very low 3 digit serial number.

    This rifle is in great condition with no rusting or pitting on metal surfaces (very slight lightening of the bluing near the muzzle, shown in pics). The hardwood stock is in excellent shape with a very nice grain and only a small scratch on the left side of the grip (shown in pics). The bore looks great with nice rifling and no rust or corrosion. Features dual aperture rear sight and hooded front post. Trigger is crisp, bolt cycles very smooth. Comes with one 10 round magazine and leather sling.

    $950 shipped and insured. EMT only. No trades please. More pics available on request.
